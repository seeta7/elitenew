Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Social Media Marketing: We will create engaging social media campaigns, manage brand presence, and build communities to enhance brand awareness and customer engagement.
Content Marketing: Our team will develop SEO-friendly content that will attract, engage, and convert target audiences.
Search Engine Optimization (SEO): We will boost websites to improve organic search rankings, increase visibility, and drive relevant traffic.
Videography and Photography: We provide guidance and expertise to clients in choosing the right visual strategy for their specific needs, helping them effectively communicate their message through visuals.
Analytics and Reporting: We provide comprehensive data analysis and regular reports to track campaign performance and identify opportunities for improvement.
We will go through the screening process with admin as the content can be sensitive & misleading if not handled properly.

Team Structure

CEO: Leads the overall vision and strategic direction of the company.
Operations Manager: Oversees day-to-day operations, project management, and client relationships.
Marketing Director: Develops marketing strategies, coordinates campaigns, and ensures alignment with business goals.
Social Media Manager: Develops and implements social media strategies, manages social media accounts, and engages with the audience.
Content Writer/Copywriter: Creates compelling and SEO-friendly content for websites, blogs, and social media.
Project Manager: Coordinates and manages client projects, ensuring timely delivery and client satisfaction.
Videographers (x4): Skilled in capturing dynamic video footage, operating cameras, and setting up equipment for different shooting scenarios.
Photographer (x4): Experienced in composing and capturing stunning images, utilizing lighting techniques, and conducting photoshoots.
